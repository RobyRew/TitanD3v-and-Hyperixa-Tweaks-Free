Description


Surge can replace the low power alert with a banner or capsule notification, you can enable Low Power Mode when your device’s battery reaches a certain percentage. Surge’s sleek and unobtrusive design allows it to fit in seamlessly with any setup and won’t disturb your workspace like the default low power alert.


Style

• You can choose between either the banner or capsule

• You can still be able to choose light or dark mode or colour if you want neither


Quick Action

Tap the battery on your statusbar to show the pop up where you can enable or disable Low Power Mode


Compatibility




Supported iPhone on iOS 14 only
Unsupported for iPad

Dependencies




• libTitanD3vUniversal
