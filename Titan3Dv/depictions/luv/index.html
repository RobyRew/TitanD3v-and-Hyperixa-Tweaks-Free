Description


Luv brings a floating button to your device for the Spotify app, when playing a song the button will appear so you can like or dislike a song, if you like a song, tap on the heart button so it will add the song to your liked collection.


Visibility

When Spotify is no longer open or no song is playing the heart button will automatically be hidden so it’s not in the way of anything.


Appearance

Make the button convenient for you by colouring it in Luv’s settings, Luv will also work with either light or dark mode.


Sizes

Choose between Small, Medium or Large for the size of the button.


Notification

When you add tracks to the liked collection or remove them from the liked collection, you will receive a notification to notify you.


Compatibility




Supported iPhone on iOS 14 only
Unsupported for iPad

Dependencies




• libTitanD3vUniversal
