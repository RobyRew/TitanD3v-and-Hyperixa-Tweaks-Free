Description


Memoji is pretty awesome right? We love using it in iMessage but it sucks that apple doesn’t allow you to use it in other apps. Avatar brings a full-fledged studio to your device where you can create Memoji/Animoji videos with more editing equipment so you can have fun with it. Record, save, share what’s not to like?


Avimoji

You can change to whether you want your profile avatar to be Memoji, Animoji, Emoji or just text, you can also choose between having a background or a coloured one. You’ll have the option to change the shape of the background from square to circle too. You can save and share this as well.


Studio

Here you can select from every Memoji and Animoji you have. Once you’ve selected you can choose to have a background from your photo library or a coloured one, to add stickers and when to start recording. When you click record you will have the option to record sound and not to. When you’ve finished recording you’ll see a preview of your video and be able to send and share it.


Library

Here you’ll see every video you’ve made with the avatar app each in the order that you made them. All videos here will be saved to your photo library too.


Settings

You can choose whether to import Memoji from iMessage or create your own in the Avatar app you can always change this in the settings section. Also if you’re having any trouble you can always contact us via social media through the settings option.


Important Message

Unfortunately the Avatar app requires devices with TrueDepth camera aka notch devices which include: iPhone X, iPhone XS, iPhone XS Max, iPhone XR, iPhone 11, iPhone 11 Max, iPhone 11 Pro Max, iPhone 12 Mini, iPhone 12, iPhone 12 pro, iPhone 12 Pro Max and on iOS 14 only!
